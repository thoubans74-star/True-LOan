Privacy Policy for True Loan

Effective Date: July 01, 2026

True Loan ("we", "us", or "our") operates the True Loan mobile application. This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which we collect, use, maintain, and disclose information collected from users (each, a "User") of the True Loan mobile application.

We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal data. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your data and how we treat it.

1. IMPORTANT DISCLOSURE: INTERMEDIATE THIRD-PARTY BROKER ROLE

True Loan is NOT a direct lender, banker, non-banking financial company (NBFC), or financial institution.

  • Platform Role: True Loan acts solely as an intermediate marketplace / third-party broker platform that connects:
    • Lenders: Individual or corporate entities who advertise available funds they wish to lend.
    • Borrowers: Individuals who list advertisements detailing the loan amount and terms they need.
  • No Direct Lending: We do not issue loans, make credit decisions, process loan payouts, calculate interest rates, or collect loan repayments.
  • Independent Agreements: All transactions, negotiations, agreements, interest rates, and loan repayments are discussed and agreed upon directly between the Lender and the Borrower. We do not participate in, control, or take responsibility for the execution or terms of these individual agreements.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

To provide a secure matching platform and verify identities, we collect the following types of information:

A. Personal Identification Information

  • Name & Contact Information: Name, email address, mobile phone number, and physical address/pincode (collected during onboarding, OTP verification, and profile setup).
  • PAN Card (KYC Verification): We collect your Permanent Account Number (PAN) to perform identity check matching (KYC status check) to prevent fraud, identity theft, and spam accounts on the platform.

B. Device & Technical Information

  • Device Identifier: Unique device ID (saved locally in SharedPreferences) to secure your account session and bind active credentials.
  • Internet Permission: Accessed via android.permission.INTERNET to connect to our secure servers to retrieve lender/borrower listings and submit profile info.

C. Location Data (Location Permissions)

True Loan requests access to location services via:

  • android.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION
  • android.permission.ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION

Why we collect location data: We access your device location to determine your city/state to filter and display local, relevant loan requests and lender advertisements near you. This ensures optimal and accurate peer-to-peer matching.

3. HOW WE USE THE COLLECTED INFORMATION

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Facilitating Matches: To list your lender/borrower advertisements on the marketplace matching boards.
  • KYC Verification: To confirm that accounts are valid and verified via their PAN, maintaining a spam-free environment.
  • Location-Based Filtering: To display relevant local advertisements near the User's geographic area.
  • Session Security: Using device IDs and session tokens to keep the user safely authenticated without repeated logins.

4. DATA SECURITY AND ENCRYPTION

We prioritize the safety of your personal data and employ advanced administrative, technical, and physical security measures:

  • Transmission Security: All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted using Secure Socket Layer (SSL/TLS) HTTPS protocols.
  • Encryption Standards: High-value data, including your PAN card details and verification metrics, is encrypted using industry-standard AES-256 encryption.
  • Storage Protection: Access to user data is strictly limited to authorized personnel and is subject to confidentiality obligations.

5. SHARING OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

  • No Selling of Data: We do not sell, trade, or rent Users' personal identification information to third parties.
  • Consent-Based Listings: By submitting a loan request or lender advertisement, you agree to make the listing details (such as your name, approximate location, loan amount, and role) visible to other registered users on our marketplace matching board to facilitate contact.

6. DATA RETENTION AND DELETION RIGHTS

  • Retention Policy: We retain your personal data only as long as is necessary to fulfill the broker/matching services or to comply with statutory legal requirements.
  • User Account Deletion: Users have the right to request the deletion of their accounts and associated personal data at any time. You can request account deletion by contacting us at: support@trueloan.ai.in

7. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

True Loan has the discretion to update this Privacy Policy at any time. When we do, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this document. We encourage Users to frequently check this page for any changes to stay informed about how we are helping to protect the personal information we collect.
